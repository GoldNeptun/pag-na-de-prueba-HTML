Bioshock 2 History

BioShock 2 takes place eight years after the events of BioShock. After Jack escaped Rapture with some Little Sisters, Dr. Brigid Tenenbaum escapes the city with the rest of the Little Sisters she had saved. The deaths of Andrew Ryan and Frank Fontaine have vacated the position of city leader. This position is filled by Dr. Sofia Lamb, Ryan's political enemy who was imprisoned for her ideas in a secret prison, far from Rapture. Lamb begins to manipulate the Splicers thanks to a sect called the Rapture Family, led by Father Simon Wales. While Ryan believed in individual genius, Lamb believed in collective effort, and frequently uses the symbol of a butterfly as the "rebirth" of Rapture.

Bioshock Infinite History

BioShock Infinite tells the story of Booker DeWitt,a war veteran of doubtful morals who must capturea young woman, Elizabeth, in the city of Columbia to pay off a debt. What Booker does not know is that the city is floating in the skies of 1912. This city is part of a very little known episode in the history of the United States, and since its isolationism, it has developed its own culture. When Booker arrives in Columbia he is going to find a welfare society based on the slavery of blacks, Irish and Chinese. Where the axis of everything is the patriarchal and dictatorial figure of the Prophet, a visionary who has not only created and founded the city, but has created a religious cult about the figure of the political fathers of the United States and of a messiah who will purify the world under Columbia with fire and sword.
